# 🚀 BEST AI VIDEO GENERATION TOOLS

## 🥇 RECOMMENDED: **Runway Gen-3**
**Why:** Best for professional marketing videos
- **Pros:**
  - Highest quality output
  - Great motion consistency
  - Text-to-video capabilities
  - Professional results
- **Cons:** Premium pricing
- **Cost:** $12-$95/month
- **Best For:** Main promotional video

## 🥈 ALTERNATIVE: **Pika Labs**
**Why:** Great balance of quality and cost
- **Pros:**
  - Good quality
  - Affordable
  - Fast generation
  - Easy to use
- **Cons:** Less control than Runway
- **Cost:** Free tier + $10/month
- **Best For:** Social media versions

## 🥉 BUDGET OPTION: **Leonardo.ai**
**Why:** Free tier with good results
- **Pros:**
  - Free tier available
  - Image + video generation
  - Good for stills
- **Cons:** Video quality lower than Runway
- **Cost:** Free + $12/month
- **Best For:** Static scenes, thumbnails
